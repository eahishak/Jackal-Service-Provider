Scholarships

Mastercard Edinburgh Scholarship

The Mastercard Edinburgh Scholarship is a prestigious program aimed at supporting exceptional students who demonstrate outstanding academic merit and leadership potential. It provides full funding for a master's degree at the University of Edinburgh, covering tuition fees, living expenses, and travel costs.

Eligibility:

  • Open to international students from eligible countries
  • Applicants must have an offer of admission to a master's program at the University of Edinburgh
  • Demonstrate strong academic achievements and leadership qualities

Application Deadline: June 30, 2023

For more information and to apply, visit the Mastercard Edinburgh Scholarship website.

African Leadership University Scholarship

The African Leadership University Scholarship is a prestigious program offered to outstanding students across Africa who demonstrate exceptional academic achievements, leadership potential, and a passion for driving positive change in their communities. The scholarship provides full or partial tuition coverage for a bachelor's degree at the African Leadership University campuses.

Eligibility:

  • Open to African nationals from all countries
  • Applicants must have a strong academic track record
  • Demonstrate leadership potential and community engagement
  • Show a commitment to driving social impact in Africa

Application Deadline: October 31, 2023

For more information and to apply, visit the African Leadership University Scholarship website.

Note: The scholarship application requires submission of academic transcripts, recommendation letters, a personal statement, and may include an interview process.
